About Student Grade System

                

                    The Student Grade System is a comprehensive tool designed to streamline the management of student information
                    and facilitate the calculation of General Weighted Average (GWA) for educational institutions. It provides
                    features for administrators to input student data, record grades, and generate reports efficiently. With its
                    user-friendly interface and robust functionality, the Student Grade System aims to enhance the academic
                    experience for both students and educators.
